Why we exist


In 2020, America experienced a devastating outbreak of COVID-19. Tracking the spread and epidemic curves of this virus at the county level is critical for effective local responses to outbreaks. We attempt to solve this problem by combining county-level socioeconomic, epidemiological, and mobility datasets with state-level case projections and testing data to train machine learning models to predict epidemic curves.
